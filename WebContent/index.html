Hi! Weâ€™re Superposition San Ramon, a science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) oriented non-profit working towards bridging the gender gap in the various fields of STEM. Our activities range from guest speaker events and workshops to fun and engaging competitions and so much more! These events are aimed towards students in grades 1-8 so that we can create a platform for young brains to be exposed to the many diverse topics in STEM. We also focus on making a difference in our community, meaning that anyone wishing to participate in our organization-held events is welcome to join and experience all the wonders that STEM has to offer. Our mission is to empower all students, especially young girls, into getting their hands dirty exploring the many aspects of STEM in an environment where they are free of stereotypes, discrimination, lack of representation, and any other obstacles that prevent them from reaching their true potential.
